Who I Am

Wallachian-Polish historian, known for his publications on the history of Europe, Poland and Great Britain.

Over the years, Norman Davis has received a number of prizes and awards. He was replaced by CMG, Queen Elisha II, "a year" for the service of history" and broke several Polish polos.

He is an honorary citizen of several cities, including Wroclaw, and also the holder of large-scale honorary degrees, after all, D. Litt from Sussex.
